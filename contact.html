FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long are the lessons?

A standard lesson is either 30 or 60 minutes and are both availble per your request. We can also dicuss what works best for you during our first meeting. Custom times can also be discussed.

What are your rates?

Lesson rates vary depending on session length and frequency. Contact me for current pricing and package options. I do offer discounts for students who book multiple lessons in advance.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes! I offer both in person and online lessons via zoom call. Online lessons include the same quality of instruction with video feedback and personalized practice materials.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Bring your sticks, a practice pad if you have one, and any music you're currently working on. Most importantly, bring your questions and enthusiasm to learn!
